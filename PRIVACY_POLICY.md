# Privacy Policy for XSweep

**Effective Date:** March 28, 2026

## Overview
XSweep ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our extension handles your data. Because XSweep operates entirely within your browser, **we do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.**

## What data is collected and how is it used?
XSweep requires the `storage` and `webRequest` permissions to function:
1. **storage**: Used strictly to save your local UI preferences (such as unfollow speed, days inactive, follower limits, and custom protected accounts) onto your own local device. 
2. **webRequest**: Used strictly to observe the X (Twitter) authentication tokens during your active session. This is required because the extension leverages the official, native X/Twitter internal APIs to fetch your following list and manage your unfollows securely on your behalf.

Your X/Twitter handle and basic user information are processed temporarily in memory as you browse to render the application user interface, but are never tracked by or transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

## Third-Party Services
We do not use analytics algorithms, trackers, or marketing endpoints. The only network requests made by this Extension are those interacting securely and directly with the X.com and Twitter.com domains using your own local browser authentication.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the functionality of the Extension changes or the Chrome Web Store rules require modification. 

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the public GitHub repository or contact the developer via X (@token_garden).
